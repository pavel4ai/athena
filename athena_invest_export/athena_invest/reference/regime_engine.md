# Regime Detection Engine

Jointly maintained by **Athena Scout** (signals) and **Athena Oracle**
(interpretation). Written to `blackboard/regime.md` and re-read by all agents.
Portfolio construction rules change automatically by regime and horizon class.

## The Nine Regimes

1. **risk-on expansion** — growth up, credit easy, equities lead.
2. **risk-off contraction** — flight to safety, equities sell, bonds bid.
3. **inflation shock** — CPI surprise high, rates up, real assets lead.
4. **deflation scare** — demand collapse, rates down, cash/duration win.
5. **commodity boom** — energy/materials lead, inflation tailwind.
6. **AI expansion** — secular tech/semis leadership, capex cycle.
7. **liquidity crisis** — funding stress, correlations → 1, cash is king.
8. **geopolitical escalation** — risk premia spike, oil/defense bid.
9. **post-conflict recovery** — risk premia unwind, cyclicals/small-cap rally.

## Required Output (regime.md)

- **Current Regime** — one of the nine, with evidence.
- **Previous Regime** — and the date/trigger of the transition.
- **Probability of Regime Change Within 30 Days** — 0–100% with drivers.
- **Watch Triggers** — specific events that would flip the regime.

## Doctrine
Identifying **regime transitions before the broader market** is the single
highest-value activity (Lesson 2). The Timur 100K project generated significant
alpha precisely by calling transitions early. Scout watches for the leading
signals (X velocity, flows, vol regime); Oracle converts them into a probability
and a second-order thesis.
